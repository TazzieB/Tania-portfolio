About Me

Not your
typical consultant.

"I've spent my career building from the inside, leading teams, crossing continents, and working alongside communities most people only ever read about. Every one of those experiences walks into the room with me."

I've spent my career on the inside of businesses. Not advising from a distance. Embedded in the work. Starting from the ground up in real estate, I moved through every layer of operations, compliance and leadership, eventually managing a team of 14. I've also grown up around business ownership. Family ventures across retail, fitness and hospitality taught me early how businesses really work and where they quietly fall apart.

What I do is simple to describe and rare to find: I walk into a business, read the room, and see clearly what others have stopped noticing. The gaps in the team, the process nobody follows, the marketing that isn't connecting, the hire that needs to happen. Then I help fix it. Not by handing over a report and disappearing, but by working alongside you to implement real change, train your people, and build something that holds.

I analyse, advise and implement. What I love most is that moment when things start to click into place for a team, when a business that felt stuck suddenly has direction and momentum. I bring energy, creativity and a genuine love of problem-solving to every project. One thing I always come back to: focus on the solution, not the problem.

Outside of work, my life has taken me to some extraordinary places. I have driven across Africa, helped build a bakery in Burundi, worked in orphanages in Nepal and South Africa, and volunteered in marine conservation. I bought my first block of land at 21, built my own home, and have owned four properties since. I have always had a thing for creating experiences too, whether that is organising a special dinner, a community event, or something unexpected that brings people together. It is the same instinct that makes me good at what I do professionally.

All of that shapes the way I work. I connect with a Balinese hostel team as easily as I sit in a boardroom in Sydney. I understand people from all walks of life because I have genuinely been among them. And I care about the businesses I work with, not just the outcomes. When I take on a project, I am really in it.
